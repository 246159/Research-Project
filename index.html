THE AESTHETICS OF VIDEO GAMES

Why do games look the way they do? for years I've wonder this myself, and up until this point I thought there was no clear answer. This is a complicated subject, but this website will try to break it down to its roots. Here you will see why displays looked the way they do, how this was translated to games and the internet, learn about the most visually groundbreaking games, and even artists that have pushed this type of art further beyond games alone
